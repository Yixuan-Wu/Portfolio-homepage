Hello Again

I’m Ivy Wu, a multidisciplinary designer specializing in User Interface Design, User Experience Design based in Toronto. I’m passionate about empowering users through beautiful, intuitive designs.

I love UX for its human-centric, multidisciplinary, and problem-solving nature and I’m challenging myself to harness all three aspects by working on different types of web & app projects, exercising empathy and stretching my boundaries. When I’m not doing UX, I enjoy travelling and cooking.

I’m currently working as a UX/UI Designer at Amazon, holden a Graduate Certificate in User Experience Design in 2019 and a BA in Interior Design in 2016 and have studied Liberal Arts and Sciences at OSU (Ohio State University) in the year of 2014.

Catch me around Toronto or send me a hello at wuyixuan577@gmail.com. I’d love to chat!
